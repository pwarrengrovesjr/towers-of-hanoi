TOWER OF HANOI

A Browser Console Game Constructed by Warren Groves

To get started, press ctrl + shift + j to open up the browser console. Each peg is represented by the three dashes - "---". And the discs are represented by the numbers, with 5 being the largest and 1 being the smallest. To play the game, type "moveDisc(), and in the parentheses first type "(1, 2)", if you'd like to move the top disc from the first peg to the second, etc. What are you waiting for? Start playing!
